## Key Points To Cover

- Total Overdose prioritized fun and style over realism.
- The "playground chaos" loop made moment-to-moment gameplay memorable.
- It blended action-comedy tone with over-the-top combat in a way few peers did.
- It lived in the shadow of bigger franchises despite a strong gameplay identity.
- Being imperfect is part of why it feels authentically PS2-era.
