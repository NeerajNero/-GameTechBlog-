# Content Strategy

## Goal

Build a durable content library that attracts long-term search traffic, supports social distribution, and creates monetization paths through AdSense and affiliate links.

## Audience

- Indian PC gamers comparing upgrades and performance tradeoffs.
- Console players looking for practical setup, accessories, storage, and display advice.
- Gaming laptop users dealing with heat, dust, FPS drops, throttling, and cooling.
- Streamers choosing routers, capture cards, mics, webcams, and settings.
- Budget-conscious buyers comparing monitors, SSDs, controllers, handhelds, and accessories.

## Core Content Pillars

| Pillar | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| PC gaming | Builds, FPS optimization, SSD upgrades, Windows settings, GPU/CPU balance |
| Console gaming | PS5, Xbox, storage expansion, controllers, displays, subscriptions |
| Gaming guides | Game settings, beginner guides, performance guides, troubleshooting |
| Gaming hardware | Laptops, cooling pads, routers, SSDs, monitors, keyboards, mice, controllers |
| Streaming | OBS setup, bitrate, routers, webcams, capture cards, audio |
| Indian gamer use cases | Pricing, availability, thermals, dust, electricity, ISP/router constraints |

## Article Types

- Buying guide: "Best X for Y in India".
- Comparison: "X vs Y for gaming".
- How-to guide: step-by-step setup or fix.
- Troubleshooting: symptom, causes, fixes, prevention.
- Explainer: technology explained through gaming use cases.
- Listicle with judgment: practical ranked recommendations.
- Evergreen guide: updated periodically with `updatedAt`.

## Publishing Priorities

1. Evergreen search articles around hardware and setup problems.
2. Practical Indian market buying guides.
3. Social-friendly guides with strong hooks and clear visuals.
4. Product comparison articles with affiliate potential.
5. Timely gaming/tech posts only when they can be updated or connected to evergreen pages.

## Quality Bar

Each article should have:

- Clear search intent.
- Specific audience.
- Practical recommendation or answer.
- Original angle or experience.
- Useful headings.
- Internal links to related articles.
- Image with descriptive alt text.
- SEO title and description.
- Disclosure if affiliate links are included.
