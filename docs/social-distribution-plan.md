# Social Distribution Plan

## Goal

Turn each article into smaller social posts that drive traffic and build a personal brand around practical gaming and tech advice.

## Channels

- X/Twitter for quick takes and threads.
- Instagram for carousel-style tips and short reels.
- YouTube Shorts for practical setup tips.
- Reddit only when genuinely helpful and aligned with community rules.
- LinkedIn for broader tech/personal brand posts when relevant.

## Per-Article Outputs

Use `templates/social-snippets.md` to create:

- 1 short hook.
- 1 X/Twitter thread outline.
- 3 short social posts.
- 1 carousel outline.
- 1 short video script idea.

## Social Angles

- Problem: "Your gaming laptop is not slow; it may be throttling."
- Comparison: "SSD upgrade vs RAM upgrade for game loading."
- Indian context: "Why gaming laptop cooling is harder in Indian summers."
- Buying judgment: "What I would avoid when buying a budget gaming monitor."
- Quick fix: "Three router settings to check before blaming game servers."

## Distribution Workflow

```text
publish article -> create social snippets -> post short hook -> post thread/carousel -> link back to article -> monitor responses -> update article if useful questions appear
```

## Rules

- Do not overpromise performance gains.
- Use article URLs with canonical links.
- Turn reader questions into future article briefs.
